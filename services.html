Purpose

Owen is organizing a completely free tutoring event for both tutors and tutees to share their knowledge and grow with each other. Whether you are passionate about teaching, struggling to score higher, wanting to gain volunteer certificates, or recieving free tutoring by selected tutees, this would be a fantastic opportunity for you. Owen will create group chats on different platforms such as Instagram, Wechat and Snapchat and add people in. Further details including time and arrangements will be addressed inside the group chat.
Note: All these group chats ban advertisements and sensitive content! It is not tolerent to violate other people's value and property.

How to register?

The way to register is very simple. Scroll down to the form, fill out necessary information. If you have further concerns or questions, type in the textarea at the bottom. For tutees, state the subject/subjects you need help with. For tutors, I would like to know about you more. There will be a short interview arranged according to your preferred time. So, please fill out your email address. I will get back to you as soon as I can.
